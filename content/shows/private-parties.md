## The Private Experience

There is a particular magic available only in intimate settings — the magic of one person looking another in the eye across a dinner table and knowing, with absolute certainty, that something impossible just happened.

Victor's private party work is rooted in this intimacy. Moving through your event as a guest — not a performer, not a spectacle — he engages individuals and small groups with effects that feel personal, tailored, and entirely inexplicable. A playing card selected in silence. A word thought and never spoken, read aloud. A guest's childhood memory surfaced with startling accuracy.

### Formats

**Strolling Close-Up** — Victor circulates through your reception or dinner, spending fifteen minutes with each group. Guests are unaware of what to expect until it begins, which creates a cascade of wonder as word spreads through the room.

**Parlour Show** — A semi-formal seated performance for groups of up to forty, ideal as an after-dinner entertainment. Running thirty to forty-five minutes, this format brings the intimacy of close-up work to a larger audience without losing its personal quality.

**Combination** — For longer events, Victor recommends opening the evening with strolling work during drinks, then closing with a brief parlour set after dinner.

### Occasions

Victor performs for birthday milestones, anniversaries, wedding receptions, engagement parties, retirement celebrations, and any gathering where the host wishes to give their guests a truly singular memory.

All private engagements are handled with complete discretion.
