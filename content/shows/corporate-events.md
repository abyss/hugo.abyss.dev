## The Corporate Experience

Victor's corporate work is built on a single conviction: the most memorable business events are the ones that make attendees feel something. Not just engaged — genuinely astonished. That spark of wonder, shared collectively across a room of colleagues and clients, creates a bond that no keynote or team-building exercise can manufacture.

His corporate programme is modular and fully adaptable. Whether he joins your event as an after-dinner headliner, a conference centrepiece, or a roving close-up performer during a reception, he shapes each appearance to complement your objectives and brand.

### What to Expect

A typical after-dinner performance runs 60 minutes and moves through three acts: opening mind-reading that draws in the room, a centre-piece demonstration involving a small number of volunteers selected from the audience, and a closing sequence that Victor describes only as "the reason people call their partners on the way home."

For conferences, Victor offers a shorter keynote-adjacent format — 20 to 30 minutes of mental feats that frame themes around decision-making, perception, and human behaviour, giving delegates a striking and memorable lens through which to revisit the day's content.

### Client Sectors

Victor has performed for clients in finance, technology, luxury goods, pharmaceuticals, legal services, and media. His material is always appropriate, never political, and designed to delight the broadest possible cross-section of a professional audience.

### Logistics

Victor supplies his own technical rider (microphone and basic lighting only), handles all travel arrangements, and provides full insurance documentation. A production liaison is available for larger events.
